Introduction

Main character is Roangyu.

Not sure if Roangyu should be a girl or boy lol.

Roangyu is actually a boy. 
The story is about an ambitious man who must defeat a giant, solve three riddles, and attend a dance to overcome three challenges. 

Things are complicated by the villain kidnapping the main character's brother.  

Assistance comes in the form of a magic swan.The magic swan speaks to humans and can see the future. It tells him where his brother is and that he'll save him if he goes for the rescue.
